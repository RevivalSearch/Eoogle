Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2023

Your Privacy Matters

We're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about what we collect.

1. Information We Collect

Eoogle collects the following types of information:

  • ECSR user IDs and usernames
  • Discord user IDs for linked accounts
  • Command usage data (for analytics and improvement)
  • Server IDs where the bot is used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Track username changes and history
  • Improve and optimize our service
  • Monitor usage of the bot
  • Detect, prevent and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored securely on our servers
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest

4. Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Users may request deletion of their data by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service, provide the service on our behalf, or assist us in analyzing how our service is used. These third parties have access to your data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to our use of your data
  • Request restriction of processing your data
  • Request transfer of your data
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact @ starlited3vv@gmail.com.
